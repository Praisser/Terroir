# Terroir
Terroir is a coffee journal app for recording brews, beans, grind size, water, tasting notes, and ratings in one place. It helps coffee drinkers track what worked, compare past cups, and slowly improve their brewing without spreadsheets or guesswork. Built as an Android app with a clean, offline-friendly flow for pour-over, espresso, and cafe notes

Live deployment: [https://terroir-three.vercel.app](https://terroir-three.vercel.app)

## What You Can Do

- Create an account with your email.
- Verify your email and choose a username.
- Log each brew with recipe details, bean origin, roast, process, flavor notes, rating, and personal notes.
- View your past brews in your journal.
- Browse community brews from other users.
- Like brews you enjoy.
- Search and explore brews by method, origin, rating, and other details.
- Follow other coffee drinkers.
- Share your public coffee profile with anyone.

## Android App

Use the Android app when you want to track coffee day to day.

1. Open the app.
2. Sign up or sign in.
3. Verify your email.
4. Pick a username.
5. Tap Log to add a new brew.
6. Fill in the brew method, dose, water, grind size, temperature, bean details, rating, flavors, and notes.
7. Save the brew to add it to your journal.
8. Use Feed to see public brews.
9. Use Explore to search and filter brews.
10. Use Profile to review your own coffee history.

## Web Dashboard

The web dashboard gives every user a public coffee profile. It is useful for sharing your coffee journey without asking someone to install the app.

Your dashboard shows:

- Total brews logged.
- Average cupping score.
- Unique coffee origins.
- Current brewing streak.
- Brew method breakdown.
- Common flavor notes.
- Recent activity.
- Public brew history.
- Follower and following counts.

To open a profile, visit:

```text
https://terroir-three.vercel.app/your-username
```

Example:

```text
https://terroir-three.vercel.app/praisser
```

## How To Use The Website

1. Visit [https://terroir-three.vercel.app](https://terroir-three.vercel.app).
2. Sign in or create an account.
3. Verify your email if asked.
4. Claim your username.
5. Add a public brew from the website or continue logging from the Android app.
6. Open your profile link to view your dashboard.
7. Share your profile link with friends or on social platforms.

## Public Profiles

Each username becomes a shareable profile page. If your username is `alex`, your public dashboard is:

```text
https://terroir-three.vercel.app/alex
```

Anyone with the link can view the public dashboard and public brews for that profile.

## Best Way To Use Terroir

Log a brew whenever you make coffee. Add enough detail to remember what changed: grind size, water amount, temperature, bean origin, process, roast, flavor notes, and rating. Over time, your dashboard becomes a simple record of what you liked, what worked, and what you want to brew again.
